About Me

Hello, my name is Andrew Kehr. I'm a software developer living in Madison Wisconsin. Currently I am a student at Thinkful working on software engineering.

I have always had an interest in math and programming and I finally have an opportunity to explore that. I have a lot of projects I want to work on and the education I receive from Thinkful will allow me to explore them.

Outside of software development I am passionate about blacksmithing and metalcraft. As with software development I love to watch a piece develop in my hands, shaping and forming it into something beautiful that will be appreciated. Here are a few things that the creative smithing process has taught me:

  1. Iterative process improvement
  2. Creating multiple designs
  3. Designing for others, not myself

With that in mind, I would love to collaborate with others in order to provide an exceptional product.
